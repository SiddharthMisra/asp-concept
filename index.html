About Us

Aravalli Sankritik Parishad is a vibrant and dedicated community celebrating the rich cultural heritage and traditions of Durga Puja. Established with the aim of fostering unity and camaraderie among members, our organization brings together individuals and families from diverse backgrounds to honor and celebrate the divine essence of Maa Durga. At Aravalli Sankritik Parishad, we believe in creating an inclusive and welcoming environment where everyone can participate in the festivities. Our community is committed to promoting cultural awareness, preserving our traditions, and encouraging active involvement in the organization of our annual Durga Puja celebrations. Each year, we strive to create a mesmerizing experience through elaborate decorations, engaging cultural programs, and community service initiatives. Our events not only highlight the artistic expressions associated with Durga Puja but also strengthen the bonds among our members and the broader community. Join us in celebrating the spirit of Durga Puja with joy, reverence, and a sense of belonging. Together, let’s make every celebration a memorable one!
